🧠 Why This Is Important

Imagine transferring money between two banks:

Without coordination:
Bank A ✔ Money Debited
Bank B ❌ Money Not Credited

❌ Inconsistent State

With Two-Phase Commit:

          Coordinator
               │
     ┌─────────┴─────────┐
     ▼                   ▼
 Service A          Service B
   Prepare            Prepare
     │                   │
     └─────────┬─────────┘
               ▼
         Commit / Rollback

Benefits:
✅ Atomic transactions
✅ Data consistency
✅ Coordinated commits
✅ Reliable distributed systems

Used By :
MySQL XA Transactions
PostgreSQL Two-Phase Commit
Oracle Distributed Transactions
Enterprise Banking Systems
ERP Platforms
